Giants Causeway

A trip on some saturday at the beginning of my stay

We went with 4 busses from Elms village, which apperently is the place I and most international students stay, to the 'The Giants Causeway'. It is basically a bridge which was build by a giant and got destroyed by another one. There are also other explanations. On the way we stopped at the Kings Road which you only know when you watch 'Game of Thrones'.

So when we arrived at the northern coast where we got fascinated by the landscape and were able to shoot awesome pictures. Here are 2 of them

After seeing this nature phenomena we went back to Belfast but this time at the coastel route. This street is soo beautiful and we saw many more places where Game of Thrones was filmed. We even saw a little of the White Wall, but you werent allowed to stop nor slow down to take pictures.
